INDY STARTUP LAB

Kick your projects in the face. Every Monday night.

★ Details ★

What is this?

A group of hackers and entreprenuers.

We meet once a week and work on whatever interests us — startups, side projects, open source, consulting, writing, or anything else that needs doing.

Who’s invited?

You. Your friends. We’re open to anyone who wants to come.

Bring your own stuff to work on or meet someone new in the group and do something awesome together.

Where and when is it?

Monday nights from 6:30pm to 9:30pm

McAlister’s Deli on 96th & Meridian

How do I learn more?

Easy—sign up for our newsletter. We'll send out infrequent updates.

Subscribe to our newsletter and/or
Follow @indystartuplab
